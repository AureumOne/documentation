## 8.1 Banks
Aureum One presents a clear, conservative, and fully documented operational structure:

- Transparent flows (fiat → gold → token → gold → fiat),
- Independent audit supervision,
- Clean compliance posture,
- Predictable transactional behavior,
- No exposure to speculative or high-risk activities.

## 8.2 Gold Suppliers & Custodians
- Aureum One performs all AML/KYC requirements independently.
- Providers face no client-level compliance involvement.
- Predictable operational demand for physical gold.
