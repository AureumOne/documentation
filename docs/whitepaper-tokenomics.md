## 6.1 Backing & Parity
- Each token represents one unit of allocated gold.
- 100% physical backing at all times.
- No issuance without confirmed physical custody.
- Strict reconciliation before and after mint/burn events.

## 6.2 Revenue Model
- Buy/sell spreads,
- Custody/demurrage fees,
- Optional premium services.

No lending, no derivatives, no leveraged exposure.
