## 5.1 Purchase Flow
1. Client submits purchase order.
2. Funds received through institutional accounts.
3. Compliance checks performed.
4. Aureum One acquires gold or allocates from inventory.
5. Custodian confirms gold position.
6. Internal allocation updated.
7. Token minted 1:1 on XRPL.
8. Auditor reviews samples and critical events.

## 5.2 Redemption Flow
1. Client requests redemption.
2. Token is burned or moved to reserve.
3. Gold is liquidated or inventory adjusted.
4. Fiat returned to client.
5. Full audit trail recorded.
