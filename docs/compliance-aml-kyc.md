sidebar_label: AML & KYC
---

The AML/KYC framework includes:

- Customer identification and risk scoring,
- Screening for sanctions and politically exposed persons,
- Transaction monitoring,
- Reporting of unusual or suspicious activity,
- Periodic review by the Independent External Auditor.

The framework aligns with international AML standards and local regulatory requirements.
