title: Independent External Auditor
sidebar_label: Independent Auditor
---

The Independent External Auditor provides:

- Oversight of AML/KYC processes,
- Verification of internal controls,
- Reconciliation of gold, token supply, and banking flows,
- Approval of critical mint/burn events,
- Preparation and validation of institutional reports.

This role ensures the governance structure remains objective, transparent, and verifiable.
