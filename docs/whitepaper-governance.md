Aureum One follows a conservative, audit-ready governance model.

## 4.1 Core Principles
- Segregation of operational, custody, and audit roles.
- Four-Eyes Principle on all critical decisions.
- Fully documented processes and audit trails.
- Independent oversight of compliance and reconciliation.
- Conservative risk posture without leverage or derivative exposure.

## 4.2 Independent External Auditor
The auditor supervises:

- AML/KYC procedures,
- Operational and financial controls,
- Gold-to-token reconciliation,
- Token mint/burn approval for critical events,
- Preparation and validation of institutional reports.
