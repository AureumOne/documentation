Aureum One's architecture consists of three strictly segregated layers:

## 3.1 Real Asset Layer (Switzerland)
- Physical gold sourced from reputable suppliers.
- Fully allocated and professionally vaulted.
- Segregated ownership and insurance.
- Custody reports and position confirmations.

## 3.2 Operational & Governance Layer (Paraguay)
- Manages client onboarding, purchase, custody representation, and redemption.
- Maintains accounting and compliance oversight.
- Coordinates all gold acquisition and liquidation.
- Supervised by an **Independent External Auditor**.

## 3.3 Digital Settlement Layer (XRPL)
- Public ledger with instant settlement.
- Transparent mint/burn processes.
- Strict 1:1 asset backing.
- Permissioned governance for token operations.
