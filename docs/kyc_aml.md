> Clear allocation of anti-money laundering and client verification responsibilities.

Aureum One **does not perform KYC or AML checks**.

**KYC and AML execution is the responsibility of custodians, execution venues, and regulated onboarding entities.**

Aureum One may **record compliance decisions, evidence approvals, and enforce rule outcomes**.
