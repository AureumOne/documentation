> Delineating what Aureum One governs and what remains with independent counterparties.

Aureum One's responsibility is **strictly limited to** governance rule enforcement, authorization workflows, institutional controls, and immutable evidence generation.

Responsibility **explicitly excluded** from Aureum One includes physical custody of gold, vault operations, trade execution, pricing, settlement of funds, KYC/AML execution, and client onboarding decisions.

Each functional responsibility remains **fully owned by** the relevant professional counterparty.
