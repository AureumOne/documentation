## Privacy & Data Protection

> How Aureum One processes, protects, and governs institutional and personal data.

Aureum One processes institutional data in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

Personal data is processed only to the extent necessary for:
- governance rule enforcement
- authorization workflows
- evidence generation
- regulatory compliance

Aureum One does not sell, share, or monetize user data.

Data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel and systems.

Clients retain ownership of their data and may request access, correction, or deletion in accordance with applicable law.

Third-party counterparties (custodians, execution venues) maintain their own privacy policies and data processing practices.

Aureum One is not responsible for third-party data processing activities.

For data protection inquiries, contact Aureum One through official channels.
